Getting Started

Wondering exactly what Symantis is and how it can help you?

Have a read over this page to get going. For more info, head over to the Docs. Symantis has 3 key components, that all work together to create a functioning digital ecosystem. The community, the Creator App and the SyKit: like in any ecosystem they exist to help each other grow and thrive.

The Community

The community is you - and every other digital creator out there, helping each other out by asking questions, providing answers and generally keeping an open dialogue between the converging worlds of design and development. To get involved, just head over to the community page and start talking! The more you contribute, the more Symantis repays you by giving you experience and control within the system.

Enhancing collaboration and communication within the web development community.

The Creator App

The Creator App is changing the way we create for the web. It is an intuitive tool that allows you to quickly and easily create production level web sites and web apps. Symantis pays attention to your strengths and weaknesses and adapts to your needs. Be it a visually led drag and drop interface, or a code based IDE, the Creator App allows you to work along the digital pipeline in a way that’s best for you. Jump in to The Creator App to get going!

Bridging the gap between Designers and Developers.

Build using Manti.

You no longer need to reinvent the wheel every time you want to create a new element for your web interface. Symantis breaks down projects into small functional pieces called Manti. Imagine a box of Legos, where each brick is a Manti. Select the piece you want and simply pop it in place in the desired location. Building websites is now this easy. Not only can you create your own Manti as you build up your web app, you can also access a vast open source library, generated by our users.

Change the way you think about building for the Web.

Work in your pipeline.

As designers and developers we know that most people move along a pipeline when working on projects. Often one person will work up until a point and then pass it on to another. The Creator App doesn’t forget this, and seamlessly integrates a digital pipeline into your workflow. Working by yourself or in a team is easy. You can see exactly what part of the project everyone is working on, move along at your own speed and even lock down sections if you don’t want to be interrupted by multiple people working on the same part of the project.

Seamlessly integrate your traditional workflow into a digital environment that's been made for you.

The SyKit

Want to be able to take your creations a step further and push the boundaries of design and development? The SyKit offers the full Symantis experience, to give you the ultimate workspace. The SyKit is your personal creation hub. With it, you can test your Manti in a live internet space. It acts as your own personal server. Not only that, but you can also connect multiple people to the one device and work together in the Creator App in real-time! Read more about it here.
