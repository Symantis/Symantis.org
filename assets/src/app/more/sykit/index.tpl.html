Overview

We’ve created the SyKit to help you get up and running with Symantis as fast as possible. If you’re not technically inclined, just plug in and go! There’s no tricky install to worry about.

Features.

  • No installation required! Just plug it in and you’re ready to go.

  • Test your Projects in a live environment, the SyKit is your own personal server.

  • Save space, store your Manti, and work on them offline.

  • SyBit Ready - let your SyKit work for you as a Symantis miner.

  • Sleek, modular design - Stack multiple SyKits and amplify your creative power.

Design.

The SyKit has been designed from the ground up with both form and function in mind, every step of the way. It is an outstanding ergonomical achievement in portable, modular computing. The combination of 3D printing and handcrafted steel provides a strong, flexible body that is not only durable, but also aesthetically stunning. Magnets allow the top and bottom plates to be easily snapped into place or removed, to make way for infinite SyKit stacking and customization. The SyKit is the ultimate Creator’s toolkit.

Technical.

The SyKit is built on the affordable computing power of a Raspberry Pi Model B+ and runs a custom version of Raspbian Linux. Each SyKit also comes equipped with a 12 GB flash drive and AC 11 wifi dongle. The top and bottom plates of every SyKit can easily be removed for endless expandability making the SyKit a perfect starting point for any homebrew project.
